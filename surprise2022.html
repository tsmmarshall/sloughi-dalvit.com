The biggest surprise of 2022!

December 11th 2022 / Lenka Kubová

Even the end of the year is in the spirit of exhibitions. There aren't that many of them, Anička and I spend more time at winter stadiums. This year she is incredibly successful and leads the figure skating rankings. Karel wins the title of Swiss Champion at the age of eighteen months, which is a huge success and I am incredibly proud of our boy. We exhibit in Slovakia, go to handling training lessons and our Casablanka wins the title of KCHCH Champion. And now it comes.....read on for a shortened version of one Christmas surprise :-)

Meddie's story.....

My aunt's Maltese died. She turned to me with a question about the little dog. I'm absolutely clueless in this area of breeding, but! I have friends, of course. A few messages, phone calls and then I stopped looking. I knew I had found it. I was not impressed by the breed itself, but by its breeder! Old guard - that's the only correct label and it says it all. About dogs, breeders, people in general. But... my aunt fell in love with another puppy, born nearby, thank God in a kennel, no breeder without proper origin. But old Mrs. Pavlíková didn't put me to sleep... why are the people you tend to like immediately, so far away? How could it possibly turn out, right... I drove across the whole country when it was already 3:0 at home, and my intuition was right. I believe that I brought home an "orange" ball as wonderful and characterful as its breeder. I bow with love and respect, and I promise on my honor that I will represent the Pavlíková name in an exemplary manner.
Welcome home, little Reny z Tichého háje, alias Meddie.
And that's the whole story about how to the family of a die-hard hound person a flea was added , sorry - the faithful shadow of our Anička, which makes our dad completely soft. And I? Every day I explain to those long-legged hunters that this is not a running bait!
