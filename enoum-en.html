Enoum ag Dalvit

Born 14.12.2023

See that little black acrobat? I will tell you something about him.

He was born third in the order, at 8:45 a.m. he made his way into the world in the rear position, with a birth weight of 550g. A beautiful little boy without a single white hair. No sovereign, as if he kept asking his mother what to do, where to find the warm milk... he earned the nickname Ňoumíček (I don't know the English equivalent...). He was a week old yesterday, and Ňoumíček is slowly turning into a prudent boy who knows how to fight and, as I add with a laugh: he will reach the finish line even on his back! But he never gives up!
