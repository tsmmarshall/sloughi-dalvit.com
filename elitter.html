E litter DALVIT

November 12th 2023 / Lenka Kubová

My breeding was born out of pure passion in the early 2000s, I knew nothing and everything seemed incredibly beautiful to me. I was lucky enough to join a club run by passionate professionals thanks to my first sloughi. The club published a well-run magazine with interesting articles, and starting there, I learned that the gene pool is the basis for any good breeder. That is why I have never sought only beauty, but have always been interested primarily in health, functionality and performance. The litter 'E' I have planned is the logical result of this choice I have made.
The mother, C'Casablanka ag Dalvit is my own breeding and, to be concise, she meets 100 percent of all the criteria I have evaluated: she is healthy, in standard, powerful and temperamental. So far, I do not know of another sloughia who has achieved so many placings on the podiums in shows finals from WORKING class. She did it at the large annual FCI Euro Sighthound Show last year and has done it in several other countries! She holds the title of BEAUTY and PERFORMANCE Champion in two countries. To use a local proverb from where we live, I would say: She matures like wine!
The sire of the litter, Ibnasch Inaam Mahanajim, was my first choice after researching a selection of three males, their pedigrees, their parents and siblings. I knew that for my goal of obtaining a sporting litter I had to turn to a "sporting stable." The breeding program of Mahanajim Breeding is excellent from my point of view. A combination of knowledge, reason and passion, a mix of old European lines, Moroccan, Tunisian, Libyan and Algerian blood. The dogs are healthy and achieve excellent results. Even at the age of seven! Ibnasch won the FCI World Coursing Championships this summer. He remained one of my favourites after I got to know him personally, seeing with my own eyes his "goal move." A strong dog in perfect condition. Well-deserved the title CHAMPION OF THE WORLD.

Ptáte se, proč ze všech chrtů miluji právě SLOUGHI?
And why Sloughs of all breeds? They are as adorable as they are beautiful. As funny as they are fast. As calm as they are agile. They are as loyal as they are slender, as friendly as they are muscular. They are so natural in their behaviour. Smart, friendly, simple. Together with other dogs, at home and on the road, you can do anything with them. They are perfect companions and best friends.
I love them!
