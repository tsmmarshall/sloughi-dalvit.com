Enzahi ag Dalvit

Born 14.12.2023

A beautiful son of athletic parents.

Successful Czech mom in the field of sports and also a show star C'Casablanka ag Dalvit and phenomenal German father, coursing World Champion 2023 Ibnasch Inaam Mahanajim. Shortly after his birth, he was given a household name: MANNEQUIN. With a birth weight of "only" 500g, he was the smallest of the entire litter. Elegant soft head with dark mask and red coat. A stunning boy, he amazed me. Of course, today I cannot swear that he will be successful in every way, no breeder can, but he has genes for a sports and exhibition career. I believe it will be of a lighter type, run like the wind, and be a great sight to behold. Definitely the most beautiful boy of the litter. That is why he got the name of the famous Arabian stallion that was imported to Morocco from Egypt.
