What do you see?

For most of you, I wouldn't have to write anything further.

I am aware that not only among the owners, but also among our judges, there are discussions about the Sloughi and other breeds. Some remember the old type and the FOUNDERS of our new ones. I myself do not want to go back to the thirties, when this type was "common" in Europe. A nice article on this topic can be found here:
https://www.windhundverband.de/rassen/sloughi/historie/?fbclid=IwAR2vxubLGAL-7-MSl7rgSq_fFqh9Hrvd1Y6yuo0Vm6Hb1X5fT7UPJTWs0so
Breeding material before and after the war was original in type. And it took many years after the war to find new breeding material. Well, we know modern history. We have lost a lot of material and the strongest hunters are almost gone. I think we need to go back a bit, switch, to regain what was lost.
